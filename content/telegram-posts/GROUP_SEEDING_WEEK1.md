# Discussion Group Seeding — Week 1

**Purpose:** Make the group feel alive from day one. Founder posts these to establish the norm that "this is a place where people talk."  
**Frequency:** 3 questions + 2 tips + daily word (already covered in WORD_OF_THE_DAY)  
**Rule:** React to EVERY member reply. Even just a 👍 or 🔥.

---

## Engagement Questions (post 3 across the week)

### Question 1 (Day 1 — Group Launch)

```
أول سؤال في المجتمع الجديد! 🏛

عرّف عن نفسك بجملة وحدة بالإنجليزي.

مثال: "Hi, I'm Mahmoud. I want to improve my speaking."

مو لازم تكون perfect — المهم تبدأ 💪

أنا أبدأ:
"Hi everyone, I'm the founder of Empire English. I built this community because I believe systems beat motivation every time."

دورك 👇
```

### Question 2 (Day 3)

```

🅱️ أقدّم على وظيفة دولية

🅳️ شي ثاني (قوله 👇)
```

## Tips with Reply Prompts (post 2 across the week)

### Tip 1 (Day 2)

```
💡 نصيحة سريعة:

لمّا تتعلم كلمة جديدة — لا تحفظها لحالها.
احفظها في جملة.

❌ "improve = يتحسّن" (بتنساها)
✅ "I want to improve my English every day." (بتفتكرها)

السبب: دماغك يحفظ السياق أحسن من الكلمات المعزولة.

🎯 جرّب الحين: خذ آخر كلمة تعلمتها واكتبها في جملة هنا 👇
```

### Tip 2 (Day 4)

```
💡 نصيحة النطق:

سجّل صوتك وانت تتكلم إنجليزي — حتى ٣٠ ثانية.

أعرف إنه محرج 😅 بس هذي أقوى طريقة تكتشف أخطاءك.

جرّب هالجملة:
"I'm getting better at speaking English every day."

نصيحة: حرف T في "getting" و "better" = صوت d خفيف (Flap T!)

"I'm geDDing beDDer at speaking English every day."

مين يجرّب يسجّل ويشاركنا؟ (حتى لو voice note هنا!) 👇
```

## Founder Engagement Rules (First 2 Weeks)

| Action | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| React to every member message | Always (👍🔥👏) | Show participation is noticed |
| Reply with encouragement | Every reply | Build habit of posting |
| Correct gently (only if asked) | When invited | Don't scare people from posting |
| Share member messages to channel | 1x/week (with consent) | Show the group is active |
| Drop bonus pronunciation tip | 2x/week | Make group feel special |
| Ask follow-up questions | Always | Keep conversations going |

### Response Templates

**When someone introduces themselves:**
> "Welcome {name}! Great start 💪 What's your #1 goal with English?"

**When someone tries and makes mistakes:**
> "Love the effort! 🔥 I understood everything. Small tip: [correction]. Keep going!"

**When someone asks a question:**
> "Great question! [answer]. Anyone else want to try answering?"

**When the group is quiet:**
> "Quick question for everyone: [simple question]. First person to answer gets a 🏆!"
